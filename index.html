Irish Whiskey Tour

Irish whiskey is experiencing an astonishingly strong renaissance around the world. From just four distilleries in 2013, there are now 18 distilleries in Ireland with many more planned. Experience these sites today and immerse youserlf in the history of Irish whiskey.

Ireland's Best Distilleries

Roe&Co Distillery Dublin

Located in Dublin’s City Centre, just 15 minutes from St. Stephen’s Green, The Teeling Distillery is perfectly located to visit while enjoying a staycation in Dublin, or a day out exploring with friends and family.

Jameson Distillery Midleton

Follow in the footsteps of the founding fathers of Jameson. A Jameson ambassador will guide you for a serving of heritage and history on the definitive Jameson tour experience - stories, craic, a comparative whiskey tasting and of course, a Jameson drink in the exact spot where it all began.

Dingle Distillery

The Dingle Distillery welcomes visitors to enjoy a tour of our facility where the entire production process, for all our products, are explained and a gin/vodka sample is provided. Our distillery tour will give you an introduction into Irish whiskey and into the Dingle Distillery.
