Irish Whiskey Tour Dublin

Irish whiskey is experiencing an astonishingly strong renaissance around the world. From just four distilleries in 2013, there are now 18 distilleries in Ireland with many more planned. Experience these sites today and immerse youserlf in the history of Irish whiskey.

Dublin's Best Distilleries

Jameson Distillery

Follow in the footsteps of the founding fathers of Jameson on the only tour of the original Jameson distillery in Dublin. A Jameson ambassador will guide you for a serving of heritage and history on the definitive Jameson tour experience - stories, craic, a comparative whiskey tasting and of course, a Jameson drink in the exact spot where it all began.

Teeling Distillery

Located in Dublin’s City Centre, just 15 minutes from St. Stephen’s Green, The Teeling Distillery is perfectly located to visit while enjoying a staycation in Dublin, or a day out exploring with friends and family.
